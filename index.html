About Me

I came up through data science.
I stayed for the product problems.

Three years at BCG GAMMA - BCG's data science and analytics practice - working on Analytics for FMCG, Telco and Retail clients across APAC and MENA. I built models, ran regressions, and wrote a lot of SQL. What I actually learned: how to tell when a number is lying, how to frame the right question before touching the data, and how to make a recommendation that sticks.

At Delivery Hero I moved from Analytics into Product Management. The BCG toolkit still runs in the background on every product decision - but now the output is a shipped feature, not a slide deck. The problems here are bigger, messier, and more fun.
